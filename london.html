London

London is the capital city of England. It is the most populous  city in the 
United Kingdom, with a metropolitan area of over 13 million  inhabitants.

Standing on the River Thames, London has  been a major settlement for 
two millennia, its history going back to its founding by the  Romans, who 
named it Londinium.
